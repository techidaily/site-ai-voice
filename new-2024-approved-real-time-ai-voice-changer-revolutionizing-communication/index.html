Step 3. Ensure that the Voice Changer toggle at the bottom of the Voicemod home window is enabled. Then, click the Voicebox tab and choose one of the sound effects. Speak to your microphone to listen, preview, and possibly change the new voice.

Step 4. If the voice is working, head to the application or browser tab where you want to apply the voice effect to start streaming. Thats it!

Note: Remember to select the Voicemod Virtual Audio Device (WDMI) on the application on which youll stream the sound effect. For example, if youre using Twitch, click Settings, choose Audio, then select this sound input device.
